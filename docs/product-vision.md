# Product Vision

## What is meapica

A platform where parents introduce their child's details (name, appearance, interests, city) and receive a fully personalized, illustrated children's book where their child is the protagonist. Printed and shipped via print-on-demand without touching inventory.

## Core Proposition

**"Menos pantallas, más historias para tocar."**

Convert your child into the protagonist of their own magical adventure. A physical, tangible book — not another screen.

## Positioning: "Artesanal Digital"

meapica is NOT "AI-generated content." It's digitally crafted storytelling with an artisanal soul:

- Watercolor-style illustrations (not photorealistic AI renders)
- Sewn spine, reinforced covers, Munken 170g cream-tone paper
- Personalized dedication, hand-feel quality
- The technology is invisible — the magic is visible

This positioning differentiates us from template-based competitors (Wonderbly, Hooray Heroes) who feel mass-produced.

## Target Users

| Segment | Profile | Motivation |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Primary** | Mothers 28-42 years, children 2-8, active on Instagram | Unique gifts, quality time, screen-free alternatives |
| **Secondary** | Grandparents | Special gifts for grandchildren |
| **Tertiary** | Aunts/uncles/friends | Birthdays, communion gifts |

## Key Differentiators

1. **Co-creation mode ("Creamos Juntos")** — Parent and child build the story together, choosing characters, plot twists, and endings. Not just personalization — it's a shared experience.

2. **Saga system** — Not a one-off purchase. Children can have ongoing adventures with the same character, building their own universe ("their own Harry Potter").
   - Linear sagas: continuous multi-book stories
   - Episodic sagas: same characters, independent adventures
   - Progression sagas: character grows across books

3. **Premium unboxing** — The physical experience matters. Adventure Packs include character letters, stickers, wooden bookmarks. The arrival IS an event.

4. **Artisanal quality** — No cheap print-on-demand feel. FSC-certified paper, traditional binding, premium finishes.

## Success Metrics (Year 1 Targets)

- Revenue: Validate product-market fit with first 100 sales
- NPS: > 70 (emotional product must generate delight)
- Repeat purchase rate: > 25% (saga model working)
- Average order value: > 40 EUR (upsell working)
- Production quality: < 2% returns/complaints
