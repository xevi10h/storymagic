# Business Model

## Pricing

| Product | Softcover | Hardcover Premium |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Book price | 34.90 EUR | 49.90 EUR |
| Printing (Gelato) | ~4 EUR | ~7 EUR |
| Shipping (Spain) | ~3 EUR | ~3 EUR |
| AI generation (text + images) | ~1.50 EUR | ~1.50 EUR |
| Payment commission (~5%) | ~1.75 EUR | ~2.50 EUR |
| **Total cost** | **~10.25 EUR** | **~14 EUR** |
| **Net profit** | **~24.65 EUR (71%)** | **~35.90 EUR (72%)** |

> Note: Pricing updated from initial estimates (29/42 EUR) to final positioning (34.90/49.90 EUR) based on artisanal premium positioning.

## Revenue Streams

### Core Product
- Personalized illustrated children's book (softcover or hardcover)

### Upsells
| Upsell | Price | Margin |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Pack Aventura Artesanal (letter + stickers + wooden bookmark) | +12.90 EUR | ~70% |
| Instant digital PDF | +5 EUR | ~95% (pure digital) |
| Second copy (discounted) | +15 EUR (softcover) | ~73% |
| Collection discount (3 books) | -20% on total | Still ~60%+ margin |
| Poster / collectible print | +8-12 EUR | ~65% |

### Future Revenue (Post-MVP)
- **Saga subscriptions:** Monthly/quarterly new chapter delivered
- **Premium adventure packs:** Branded packaging with physical extras
- **Gift cards:** Pre-purchased story credits
- **B2B:** Schools, libraries, event packages (birthdays, communions)

## Break-Even Analysis

**Fixed monthly costs:** ~40-55 EUR
**Variable cost per book (avg):** ~12 EUR
**Average selling price:** ~42 EUR (blended softcover/hardcover)
**Contribution margin per book:** ~30 EUR

**Break-even:** ~2 books/month (covers fixed costs)
**Target for viability:** 20+ books/month = ~600 EUR/month net profit
